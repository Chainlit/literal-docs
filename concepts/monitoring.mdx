---
title: Monitoring
---

Literal has a Dashboard on which you can monitor usage of your application. Analytics are shown in various graphs, displaying users, thread count, token usage, feedback scores experiment scores, lateny and throughput. This enables you to measure quality, cost and latency of your current setup.
